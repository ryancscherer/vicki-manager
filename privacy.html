Privacy Policy

App: Vicki the Soccer Manager
Developer: R Scherer
Last updated: June 2026

Data Collection

Vicki the Soccer Manager does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no login, and no server-side storage.

Data Storage

All data entered into the app (player names, match settings, GK history, scores) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage (cookies). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Internet Access

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts on first use. No personal data is sent in these requests. After the first load, the app functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use advertising, analytics, tracking, or any third-party SDKs.

Children

The app is designed to be used by sports coaches and does not collect data from or about children.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact: schererdeveloper@gmail.com
